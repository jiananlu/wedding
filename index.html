Welcome to Our Wedding

Here you are! You can't help open the link in our invitation card, can you? :0) Welcome! We're so happy you are here and will join our wedding! Please allow us some words for the beginning.

We didn't plan to have a "wedding" several months ago; however, as things often happen, we end up a wedding. A WEDDING!! That terrifies a lot of people (including Jianan). Preparing a wedding is an exhausted work (we can't count how many weekends we spent). But we also get something in the whole process. We learn each other better; we learn how to deal with all kinds of people; we laugh; we worry; we bite each other. Fianlly we decide to enjoy it because as we said in our invitation: where there is life, there is love.

So we hope you can also enjoy our wedding, our dear friends. It's a party to celebrate. Enjoy yourselves!

Ceremony

The ceremony will be hosted at Valley Presbyterian Church. We will start seating guests (you!) around 9:30 to 9:45. The ceremony will begin sharply at 10:00AM (We will start walking into the church nervously). So you're encouraged to arrive on time. A pretty lady will play harp to welcome you.

The whole process will last about 20 to 30 minutes (hopefully it's short enough to keep you awake). After the ceremony is over, we'd like to have you stay a little while to take pictures with us. We'll print large pictures (with you in) and hang you in our home for years :-)

We'd like to give our vow in Chinese; however it will be relative hard for the pastor to understand and approve our marriage. As a result, we decide to use English first (for the pastor) and then speak Chinese as real.

The church is not too hard to find. It's in a woods; so don't be afraid when you find you're surrounded by redwood trees. This map might be helpful for you: map. When you see this sign, that is the church!

For you to park? According to our observation, there're plenty of parking spaces in the parking lot of the church. So don't worry about it.
